Research Experiences
  • Minimax Testing Radius for Single Index Model. Advisor: Dr. Yuting Wei, Department of Statistics and Data Science, the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 03/2022 - 10/2022.
    • Proposed a reparametrization method for determining the order of minimax testing radius, inspired by techniques of minimax estimation in monotone-linked single index model.
    • Derived a lower bound of minimax radius respectively for testing problems of whether the parameter equals zero and whether it equals a specific point lying in the unit sphere.
    • Obtained an asymptotic upper bound of minimax testing radius for known Lipschitz continuous link functions via Generalized Likelihood Ratio Test.
    • Please see the report on the estimation problem on the single index model here.
  • Homogeneous Pursuit in β Model. Advisor: Dr. Canhong Wen, Department of Statistics, USTC, 03/2022 - Present.

    • Developed a primal-dual algorithm to accurately estimate parameters in the β model and obtained a linear convergence rate combined with the surrogate function.
    • Proved that the GIC proposed can asymptotically identify the tuning parameter with probability tending to one. The whole algorithm with the fine-tuning parameter achieves higher accuracy.
    • Output one manuscript in progress and see the report of previous work here.
